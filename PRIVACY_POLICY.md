# FieldScope for Salesforce — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 4, 2026

## Overview
FieldScope for Salesforce is a Chrome extension that analyzes picklist field usage in Salesforce orgs. This policy explains how the extension handles data.

## Data Collection
FieldScope for Salesforce does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or Salesforce data to external servers.

## How It Works
- The extension reads your Salesforce session cookie (sid) solely to authenticate REST API requests to your own Salesforce org.
- All analysis is performed locally in your browser in real-time.
- No data leaves your browser.
- No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is used.
- No user accounts or sign-ups are required.

## Permissions Used
| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Cookies | Read Salesforce session cookie for API authentication |
| Tabs | Detect active Salesforce tab and identify org domain |
| Storage | Persist session info across browser service worker restarts |
| Host permissions (*.salesforce.com) | Make describe and SOQL API calls to your own Salesforce org |

## Data Storage
- Session data (org URL only) is stored temporarily in Chrome's session storage and is cleared when the browser closes.
- No Salesforce record data, field values, or user information is ever persisted.

## Third Parties
No data is shared with any third party. No external servers, APIs, or services are contacted other than your own Salesforce org.

## Children's Privacy
This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
